What is this website about?
This website is a cool text game. You create a game with the command CreateNewGame and entering the number of rows and columns of the desired gameboard(rooms). You start from a central room. Every room might have a closed chest, monster or the Holy Grail. You can open the chest, which has different items in it or fight a monster. You win once you discover the Holy Grail. You loose once you lost all your HitPoints.
How can I start playing?
first you need to signup: select a 'crawler' name and give your email address. After that you need to click the confirmation link received from us in you mail box. This is for security reasons. After that you can click on 'signin' link and enter your credentials. A new screen shows up with a button start a new game. Enjoy it!
How do you calculate the score of a completed game?

For each:

* gold piece are given 50 points

* health point are given 100 points

* opened door you loose 50 points

What do you show on the score board?
We show the top five games with the highest score
