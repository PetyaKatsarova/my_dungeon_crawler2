What is this website about?
This website is a cool text game. You create a game with the command CreateNewGame and entering the number of rows and columns of the desired gameboard(rooms). You start from a central room. Every room might have a closed chest, monster or the Holy Grail. You can open the chest, which has different items in it or fight a monster. In the chest you could find nothing, a trap, a weapon or gold pieces. You win once you discover the Holy Grail. You loose once you lost all your HitPoints.You loose hit points by fighting a monster or from a trap.
How do you calculate the score of a completed game?

For each:

* gold piece are given 50 points

* health point are given 100 points

* opened door you loose 50 points

What do you show on the score board?
We show the top five games with the highest score
